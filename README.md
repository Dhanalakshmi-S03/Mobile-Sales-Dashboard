PROJECT TITLE:
Mobile Sales Analysis Dashboard – Power BI
PROJECT OVERVIEW:
This dashboard provides a complete analysis of mobile phone sales across different Indian cities, mobile models, brands, customer ratings, and payment methods. It helps sales teams understand purchasing patterns, monthly demand, and customer satisfaction trends.
KEY METRICS:
•	Total Sales: 769M
•	Total Quantity: 19K
•	Total Transactions: 4K
•	Average Rating: 4.99
DASHBOARD FEATURES:
1.	Total Sales by City (Map View):
A geographic map showing sales hotspots across cities like Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Ranchi, and more.
2.	Total Quantity by Month:
A line chart that highlights monthly demand patterns from January to December.
3.	Sales by Mobile Model:
Visualization showing the performance of models such as iPhone, OnePlus, Samsung, Vivo, and Xiaomi.
4.	Transactions by Payment Method:
A pie chart comparing UPI, Debit Card, Credit Card, and Cash transactions.
5.	Customer Ratings Analysis:
Bar chart showing rating distribution from 1 to 5 stars.
6.	Total Sales by Day Name:
Trend chart showing sales behavior across days of the week.
7.	Brand Sales Table:
Displays brand-wise total sales, total quantity, and transaction count.
TOOLS USED:
•	Power BI Desktop
•	Power Query for cleaning and transforming sales data
•	DAX for calculation of KPIs and trend metrics
